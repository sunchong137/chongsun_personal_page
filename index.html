     Hi, my name is Chong Sun, a theoretical and computational chemist. My research focuses on describing and understanding microscopic behaviors of molecules and condensed matter materials with numerical simulations. I believe in the power of computing, and I strive to push the limit of our computational capabilities by combining numerical methods, high-performance programming, machine learning and quantum computing.
